The Demon Slayer Corps (鬼殺隊) are a group of Demon Slayers that have dedicated their existence to protecting humanity from Demons in the Kimetsu no Yaiba (Demon Slayer) series. It is an organization that has existed since ancient times. The organization is made up of hundreds of Demon slayers, but the government is also not officially recognized

To become a Demon Slayer, a person must pass the Final Examination, which is an entry test to enter the Slayer Corps of Demons.

Once a Demon Slayer has integrated into the organisation, they are given the Mizunoto initial rating and must perform a variety of missions before becoming promoted. Many demon slayers receive breath styles taught to conduct their missions efficiently.

Hashira

The Hashira are the pillars of the Demon Slayer Corps. They are it's strongest warriors, who aim to eradicate all the demons in order to protect humanity. This group is under the direct control of Kagaya Ubuyashiki, and he only allows the strongest of Demon Slayer's to enlist in its ranks.

Hashira Pillars of Our Corps

The Hashira, also known as "Pillars," are the strongest fighters in the Demon Slayer Corps the crème de la crème, as they say. Slotted above the ostensibly highest ranking of Kinoe, the Hashira are the very best at what they do.

Even if something does happen, my heart will never waver. We are the nine strongest swordsmen in our organization.

Every ambitious slayer dreams of becoming an apprentice to one of the venerable swordsmen a Tsuguko and succeeding in rising to the highly respected top rank.

Demons

Demons are a carnivorous, vampire-like species whose primary diet is humans. They are also nocturnal as they are weak to sunlight, though they also stay up during the day as they don't require sleep..

Upper Rank Demons

The most elite demons belong to the "12 Kizuki" group commanded by the Demon King himself, Muzan. There are two sections within the 12 Kizuki called the Upper and Lower Moon.

Kokushibo, the strongest and the first upper rank demon, who was a former demon slayer and the twin brother of Yoriichi.

Akaza, the third upper rank demon, who was a martial artist and had destructive shockwave abilities.
